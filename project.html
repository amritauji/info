Fruits

Apple:

An apple is a delicious fruit with a crisp texture and a sweet or tart taste.

Benefits:
- Supports lung strength.
- Promotes heart health.
- May help with asthma.
- Supports bone health.
- Aids in weight loss.
- Supports brain health and memory.
- Boosts the immune system.
- Promotes gut health.

Mango:

The mango is a tropical fruit known for its juicy and flavorful flesh.

Benefits:
- Rich in vitamins and minerals.
- Supports digestion with enzymes.
- Provides antioxidants for immune health.
- May have anti-inflammatory properties.
- Supports eye health with vitamin A.

Strawberry:

Strawberries are small, red berries that are often eaten fresh or used in desserts.

Benefits:
- Packed with vitamin C for immune support.
- Rich in antioxidants.
- Supports heart health.
- Contains fiber for digestion.
- May have anti-inflammatory effects.

Orange:

Oranges are citrus fruits that are rich in vitamin C and have a tangy flavor.

Benefits:
- Excellent source of vitamin C.
- Supports immune system.
- Contains antioxidants.
- Supports skin health.
- Aids in digestion.

Banana:

Bananas are elongated and curved fruits with a sweet and creamy flesh.

Benefits:
- High in potassium for heart health.
- Rich in vitamins B6 and C.
- Contains dietary fiber for digestion.
- Provides energy.
- May help regulate blood sugar levels.

Grapes:

Grapes are small, juicy fruits that come in various colors and are often enjoyed as a snack.

Benefits:
- Rich in antioxidants.
- Supports heart health.
- Contains vitamins and minerals.
- Supports brain health.

Watermelon:

Watermelon is a hydrating and refreshing fruit known for its sweet and juicy flesh.

Benefits:
- High water content for hydration.
- Rich in vitamins A and C.
- Contains antioxidants.
- Supports skin health.

Pineapple:

Pineapple is a tropical fruit with a sweet and tangy flavor, often enjoyed fresh or in beverages.

Benefits:
- Contains bromelain enzyme for digestion.
- Rich in vitamin C and manganese.
- Supports immune system.
- May have anti-inflammatory effects.

Peach:

Peaches are fuzzy fruits with a sweet and fragrant flesh that can be enjoyed in various dishes.

Benefits:
- Rich in vitamins A and C.
- Contains dietary fiber.
- Supports skin health.
- May have anti-inflammatory effects.
